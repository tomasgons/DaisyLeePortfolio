About

Welcome to the wondrous world of Miss Daisy Lee. If you're on this page you're clearly looking for something more unique and other worldly than H&M! For the unitiated, Daisy Lee is your first stop for affordable bespoke clothing and accessories. This formidable lady is on a mission to bring back old fashioned glamour to everyday life. Hailing from a long line of inventors, ingenuity is simply in her blood. Daisy uses vintage material and recycled objects to create costumes from every era. So, if your into (Neo) Burlesque, Pin-up, Vintage, Steam Punk or just the weird and the wonderful, then look no further, she is simply the complete Vaudevillian package!
